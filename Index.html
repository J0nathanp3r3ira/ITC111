Kurt Cobain

I'd rather be hated for who I am, than loved for who I am not.

  • 1967:Kurt Cobain was born on February 20 in Aberdeen, Washington.

  • 1985-86:In 1985 Kurt left Aberdeen for Olympia where he formed the band Nirvana in 1986.

  • 1989: In 1989 Nirvana recorded their debut album Bleach under the independent label Sub-Pop records.

  • 1994: After a successful career Kurt Cobain passed away, the cause was suicide.
